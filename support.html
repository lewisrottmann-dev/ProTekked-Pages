ProTekked Support

ProTekked helps users build local security audit reports from checklist findings, evidence notes, and client details.

Common Questions

Do I need an account?
No. ProTekked is designed to work without a login.

Where is my audit data stored?
Audit data is stored locally on your device.

How do I restore purchases?
Open the Store screen and tap Restore.

Does ProTekked scan networks?
No. ProTekked organizes audit observations and report content. It is not a scanner or penetration testing tool.
