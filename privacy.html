ProTekked Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Overview

ProTekked is a local-first security audit report app. It helps users create audit checklists, evidence notes, client fields, and report content on their device.

Data Stored By The App

ProTekked stores audit information locally on the user's device. This may include client names, scope notes, checklist selections, evidence notes, and report branding fields entered by the user.

Data We Do Not Collect

ProTekked does not require an account, does not operate a user database, and does not upload audit content to a ProTekked server.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple or Google through their store systems. The app uses purchase status only to unlock paid features.

Sharing Reports

When a user exports or shares a report, the user chooses where that report goes through the device share sheet or browser download flow. ProTekked does not receive a copy.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the ProTekked developer at the support email listed on the App Store product page.
